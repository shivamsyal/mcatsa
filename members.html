TSA Technology Honor Society

If you have maintained good grades, participated in TSA and attended a national conference, gotten 3rd place or better at States or Nationals, hold a leadership position in any club, and have participated in a community service project you are eligible to apply to be a member of the TSA Technology Honor Society.

Bob Hanson Distinguished Student Award

This prestigious award is awarded with a $500 honorarium and any person can apply or be nominated that meets the following criteria:

  1. - Are members in good standing with TSA for a minimum of one school year
  2. - Have actively participated in TSA at the local, state, and/or national level
  3. - Have achieved prominence and distinction in TSA and within his or her community

William P Elrod Memorial Scholarship

This scholarship, $2,500, is open to TSA seniors and alumni who have been active TSA members for two or more years. They must submit an essay, resume and three letters of recommendation.

NJTSA State Office

Running for a position in TSA State office means you be directing and organizing the TSA state conference. These positions are hard to receive and will definitely be time consuming.

National TSA Office

Running for National TSA means you will be designing the organizing the compeittions rather than competing in them. The competition is rigorous however attainable for a dedicated TSA student from MCA. All of the rules for applying are listed in the link above.
